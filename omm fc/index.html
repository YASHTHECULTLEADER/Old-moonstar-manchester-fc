Welcome

OLD MOONSTAR MACHESTER
FOOTBALL CLUB

OMM FC is a professional football club located in (Opposite Dev
Bungalows Airport Road, Nana Chiloda Rd, Kotarpur, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 382361).
The club was founded in 2022 and has since established itself
as a competitive team in the local football scene.
OMM FC is known for its skilled players and strong team spirit. The
team is comprised of dedicated athletes who are passionate about the
game and committed to achieving success on the field.
OMM FC's success is due in part to its talented players. The team's roster
includes several standout athletes who have distinguished themselves on
the field. As a club, OMM FC is committed to promoting the sport of football in the
local community.The team regularly hosts events and participates in
charitable initiatives that support youth development and community
outreach.With its winning record, skilled players, and commitment to community,
OMM FC is a team to watch in the local football scene.
